Process

How VeriFace works

A clean three-step pipeline from upload to verdict, powered by your Express + Supabase + custom ML architecture.

01
Upload & Auth

Sign in with Clerk. Your account syncs to Supabase via webhook instantly. Drag, drop, paste, or browse any image up to 10 MB. The file is uploaded directly to Supabase Storage via the /upload endpoint.

02
ML Pipeline Runs

The Express/TypeScript backend passes the image to the VeriFace ML model. Frequency analysis, pixel entropy checks, and generation artifact detection run in parallel — returning a confidence score in under 2 seconds.

03
Verdict & Share

Results are stored to Supabase and displayed with a confidence gauge. Your scan history lives in your dashboard. Each result gets a unique shareable URL with SSR-generated OG images for social previews.
